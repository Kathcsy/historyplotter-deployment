About History Plotter

History Plotter is a website created by a group of CIT students under the College of Computer Studies department.
History plotter, as the name states, is all about plotting History. It is a website to help teachers, particularly History teachers, create materials that will be less of a mess and headache for themselves and create something that will keep the students interested in the subject.



Why a History Lesson Maker?

Studying History requires a lot of memorization for the student and a lot of collation and organizing of events for the teachers. There are also too many notes and documents to organize and discuss. History Plotter is a website that will help create interactive materials that both help the teachers and students in the studying and teaching of the History Subject.





For the usage of this website, our goal is to try to make it in a way that will attract History teachers from a high school to college level.

How it started


This idea started out as a novel writing website. It sprung as a common issue among us that as writers, it can often be such a pain to organize all the ideas of a story, especially when writing without proper experience and just as a hobby. But as it turns out, it wasn’t a very novel idea. We found all sorts of novel writing software online, most of them as a service. So, there was nothing more we could find to improve on. Because of this, we started exploring other paths when we came across the idea of lesson materials.
